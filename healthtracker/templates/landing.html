For ACOs & other Healthcare Providers

We make it easy for you to help your elderly and chronically ill patients manage their illnesses, preempt costly emergencies, and feel better. You provide your expertise, and we'll do the rest.

Increase Adherence

Patients visit the doctor to feel better and you do everything you can to help. But we know that it can sometimes be difficult to keep patients on track with their treatment. HealthTracker makes it easy to maintain cost-effective contact with patients to increase adherence while teaching them about how their daily choices affect their health over time.

Improve Outcomes

Adherence is crucial to improve your patients' outcomes, but there's more we can do. Your doctors will be able to track their patients' health in real-time. Our Smart Alert system will notify doctors of declining (or improving health), and enable you to reach out proactively to adjust their treatment and prevent additional costs down the line. The more you know, the better you can take care of your patient population.

Involve Your Patients

Patients trust their doctors with decisions that impact quality of life. Now you can show them how their lifestyle choices and treatment plan affect how they feel. Chronically ill patients live with illness every day of their lives; now they can see how each day is relative to the previous, and take more of an active role to improve their health. It's easier to make difficult lifestyle choices such as sticking to a diet when you can see the results. HealthTracker makes those results visible.
We will work with you to create a solution that just works.
