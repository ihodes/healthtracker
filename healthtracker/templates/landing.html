For ACOs & other Healthcare Providers

Increase patient adherence

Patients want to get better, and making them feel better is what your doctors are trying to accomplish. But we know that it can sometimes be difficult to keep patients on track with their treatment. HealthTracker makes it easy to maintain cost-effective contact with patients, increasing adherence while teaching them about how their healthare plan is affecting their health, even when it can be hard to see.

Improve health outcomes

Adherence is a very important step in improving your patients' outcomes, but there's more we can do. Your doctors will be able to keep up with their patients' health in real-time. Our Smart Alert system will notify doctors of declining (or improving health), and enable you to reach out in time to prevent to tweak their treatment in time to prevent a costly emergency intervention. The more you know, the better you can take care of your patient population.

Provide more value

Your patients trust their doctors with their lives, every day. Now you can show them out their lifestyle choices and treatment plan affects how they feel. Chronically ill patients are living with their illness every day of their lives; now they can see how each day is an improvement on the previous, and take more of an active role in improve their health. It's easier to make difficult lifestyle choices such as sticking to a diet when you can't see the results. HealthTracker makes those results visible.
We will work with your providers and your administrators to create a solution that fits your patients' needs. Learn more about what HealthTracker can do.
